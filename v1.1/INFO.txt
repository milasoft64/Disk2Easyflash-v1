Disk2EasyFlash v1.1 (unofficial)
Original Logic: Alex Kazik
Enhancements: Milasoft

Updates: v1.1
Allows cartridge loads from Basic LOAD command, not just Kernal loads.


Allows the creation of Easyflash cartridges containing multiple disk images.

Instructions:
https://github.com/milasoft64/Disk2Easyflash-v1/

Quick instructions:
python disk2easyflash.py
or
disk2easyflash.exe

- Allows drag and drop
- Allows over 26 disk images (space permitting)
- Improved bugfixes
